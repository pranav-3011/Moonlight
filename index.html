About Us

We are a group of college students, who have fortunately been strong enough to heal our mental and psycological bruises. And so we care about anyone else in the world, especially the young children and teens who could not gather the support and strength to fight their mental disturbances. Our journies are different, but not the obstacles. We are here to listen to you and guide you in darkness, like the moonlight, with the experience that we have gained, as your friends, or elder sisters and brothers.


We are not professionals but are just sharing our experience with you. If you have been dealing with severe problems or persistent suicidal thoughts, we strongly recommend you seeking help from a trained therapist.
